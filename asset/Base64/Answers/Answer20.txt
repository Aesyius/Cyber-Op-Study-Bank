Best practices for using Base64 encoding effectively and securely in software development and data handling include: ●Using Base64 encoding only when necessary for data transmission or storage that requires text- based representation. ●Applying encryption to sensitive data before Base64 encoding for confidentiality and security. ●Understanding the limitations of Base64 encoding, especially regarding data size and efficiency. ●Avoiding encoding sensitive information in URLs or publicly accessible locations without additional security measures. ●Regularly reviewing and updating security practices related to data encoding and protection.
