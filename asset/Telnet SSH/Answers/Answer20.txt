SSH-related security vulnerabilities include: ●Brute-force attacks: Attackers attempt to guess SSH passwords or keys through repeated login attempts. ●Key compromise: Unauthorized access can occur if SSH private keys are compromised or stolen. ●Misconfigured SSH settings: Weak configurations, open SSH ports, and improper access controls can lead to security breaches. ●Protocol vulnerabilities: Known vulnerabilities in SSH protocol implementations may be exploited by attackers.
