Different types of network diagrams commonly used in IT environments include: ●Logical Network Diagrams: Represent the logical layout of network components, such as IP addresses, subnets, VLANs, and routing protocols. ●Physical Network Diagrams: Illustrate the physical placement of devices, cables, racks, and infrastructure components like switches, routers, servers, and workstations. ●Network Topology Diagrams: Show the overall network topology, including the arrangement of devices, connections, and communication paths.
