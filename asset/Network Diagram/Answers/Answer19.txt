Challenges or limitations associated with creating and managing network diagrams include: ●Complexity in documenting large-scale networks with numerous devices, connections, and configurations. ●Maintaining diagrams accurately as networks evolve or undergo changes. ●Ensuring consistency across diagrams and avoiding outdated or conflicting information. ●Accessibility and sharing of diagrams among team members, especially in distributed environments. ●Balancing detail and complexity to create diagrams that are both comprehensive and understandable.
