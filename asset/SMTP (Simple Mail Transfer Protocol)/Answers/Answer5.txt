SMTP commands play a crucial role in the email sending process: ●HELO or EHLO: Identifies the sending SMTP server to the receiving SMTP server. ●MAIL FROM: Specifies the sender's email address. ●RCPT TO: Specifies the recipient's email address. ●DATA: Transmits the email content, including the message body and any attachments. ●QUIT: Closes the SMTP connection after the email transmission is complete.
