Best practices for using TShark effectively and securely in a network environment include: ●Understanding the network protocols and traffic patterns relevant to your analysis. ●Using appropriate filters to focus on specific traffic of interest and reduce noise in captures. ●Regularly updating TShark and associated libraries to benefit from bug fixes and security patches. ●Implementing secure handling practices for captured data, including encryption and access controls. ●Monitoring system resources when capturing and analyzing large volumes of network traffic.
