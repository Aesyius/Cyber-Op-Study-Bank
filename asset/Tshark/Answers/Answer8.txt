Examples of using TShark to capture specific protocols or traffic patterns include: ●Capturing HTTP traffic on port 80: tshark -i eth0 -f "tcp port 80" ●Capturing DNS queries and responses: tshark -i eth0 -f "udp port 53" ●Capturing VoIP traffic (e.g., SIP or RTP packets): tshark -i eth0 -f "udp portrange 5060-5061 or udp portrange 10000-20000" ●Capturing packets with specific IP addresses: tshark -i eth0 -f "host 192.168.1.100"
