The main features and capabilities of TShark include: ●Command-line interface for efficient packet capture and analysis. ●Support for capturing and dissecting a wide range of network protocols. ●Filtering capabilities to capture specific packets based on criteria. ●Protocol decoding and dissection for detailed analysis of packet contents. ●Statistical analysis tools for generating reports and summaries. ●Real-time capture and analysis capabilities. ●Ability to read and write capture files in various formats.
