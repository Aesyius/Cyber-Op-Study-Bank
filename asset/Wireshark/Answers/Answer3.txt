The main features and capabilities of Wireshark include: ●Live packet capture and analysis. ●Support for a wide range of protocols and network technologies. ●Packet filtering and display filters for focusing on specific traffic. ●Protocol decoding and dissection for detailed analysis of packet contents. ●Color-coded packet visualization for easier identification of different types of packets. ●Deep packet inspection capabilities for analyzing packet payloads and application data. ●TCP stream analysis for understanding TCP conversations and sessions. ●Statistics and graphs for analyzing network performance and traffic trends. ●Exporting captured packets for further analysis or reporting. ●Customizable profiles and configurations for different analysis scenarios.
