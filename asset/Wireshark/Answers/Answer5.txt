Common use cases for Wireshark include: ●Troubleshooting network connectivity issues, such as identifying packet loss or latency. ●Analyzing network protocols for compliance and performance optimization. ●Detecting and diagnosing network security incidents, such as malware infections or suspicious activity. ●Monitoring network traffic for bandwidth usage, application performance, and user activity. ●Analyzing VoIP (Voice over Internet Protocol) traffic for quality of service (QoS) assessment.
