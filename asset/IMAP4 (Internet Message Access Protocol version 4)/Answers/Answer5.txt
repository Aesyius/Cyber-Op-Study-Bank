Some advantages of using IMAP4 over POP for email access include: ●Email synchronization: IMAP4 synchronizes email actions across multiple devices, ensuring consistency in email management. ●Server-side storage: Emails remain stored on the server, allowing access from any device with IMAP support. ●Folder management: IMAP4 supports folder creation, organization, and management on the server, enhancing email organization capabilities. ●Message flags and status: IMAP4 supports flags for marking messages as read, unread, flagged, etc., providing additional message management options.
