CyberChef provides a wide range of built-in operations for various data processing tasks, including: ●Encoding and decoding operations (base64, hexadecimal, URL, ASCII, etc.). ●Hashing operations (MD5, SHA-1, SHA-256, HMAC, etc.). ●Compression operations (Gzip, Deflate, Bzip2, etc.). ●Conversion operations (binary to text, text to binary, JSON to XML, etc.). ●Manipulation operations (substring, regex, replace, sort, split, etc.). ●Visualization operations (heatmap, frequency analysis, bar chart, etc.).
